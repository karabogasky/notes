Relative positioning

We can position an element relative to its normal position.

The red box is static.

We have relatively positioned the green box. Its x-axis is offset by left: 50px; (which moves it left to right) and y-axis by top: -50px; (which moves it from bottom to top).

We have also relatively positioned the blue box. Because we gave it no x- or y-axis property values, the blue box acts as if it were static.
